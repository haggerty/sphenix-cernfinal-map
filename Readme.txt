Geometry corrected data for the sPHENIX mapping for the three maps:

1. Full field, 10 cm step, 10 degrees
2. Full field, 2 cm step, 10 degrees
3. Half field, 10 cm step, 10 degrees. 

The columns read as follow:

[x, y, z, |B|, bx, by, bz]

With positions in mm, b components in Tesla.

The frame of reference is the one defined by the sPHENIX surveyor.

Happy analysis!
